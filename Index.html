For Black people, you may find yourself more hesitant to travel to foreign countries than your white conterparts. You may even refuse to travel at all because of how hostile some locations around the world can be to black people. I am here to assure you that the world is a very big place and there are places where it is far safer than countries like America for black people to travel to and live in. Places like Singapore, Japan, Iceland, and many others have been reported by black expats as very safe. Some even say that their quality of life was elevated by moving there.
